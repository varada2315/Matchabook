THE DAILY RITUAL

Whisking a Moment of Serenity

Preparing matcha is more than making a drink—it is a centuries-old mindfulness practice. By taking three minutes to warm your bowl, sift your tea, and whisk it into a smooth, frothy green elixir, you create a dedicated boundary of peace in your day.

Our Ceremonial Grade pouch is designed to stand beautifully in your kitchen, serving as a daily invitation to slow down, breathe, and savor a peaceful moment amidst the chaos.

How to Prepare the Perfect Bowl

Choose the ideal grade for your daily ritual and learn how to prepare each bowl mindfully.

1. Ceremonial Grade Ritual

Stone-ground from first-flush spring leaves. Sift 1-2g into a warm bowl, add 60-70ml of hot water (70-80°C), and whisk in a quick W-motion using a chasen (bamboo whisk) until a thick, velvety green froth forms. Best enjoyed pure.

2. Signature Latte Blend

A balanced, smooth second-harvest matcha. Sift 2g of matcha, whisk with 50ml of warm water until fully smooth, and pour over 150ml of your favorite cold or steamed milk (oat milk is highly recommended) for a creamy, delicate cup.

3. Everyday Culinary Grade

Ground from later harvests, offering a robust flavor profile. Sift 2g of matcha directly into breakfast smoothies, protein shakes, or daily baked goods to add a clean, calm energy boost to your busy morning.

MATCHA EDUCATION

The Matcha Guide

Not all matcha is created equal. The grade, processing, and harvesting method determine the flavor profile and how the tea should be prepared. Discover the perfect grade to fit your daily ritual.

  • Ceremonial Matcha

    Stone-ground from the youngest first-harvest leaves. Exceptionally smooth, sweet, and delicate. Best enjoyed pure, whisked with warm water.

  • Signature Matcha

    A balanced, smooth, and versatile second-harvest blend. Rich, mellow, and full-bodied. Ideal for premium lattes and iced matchas.

  • Everyday Matcha

    Bold, spirited, and budget-friendly. Sourced from later harvests, providing a robust flavor that cuts through ingredients. Great for daily baking, smoothies, and recipes.
